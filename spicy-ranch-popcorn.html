You’re just four ingredients away from snacking paradise with this delicious Spicy Ranch Popcorn! So addicting, this delightfully easy recipe is sure to become a new family favorite!

We’re currently on a Disney cruise (I know, poor us). For the past few weeks I’ve been telling the boys about our itinerary, flying out of San Francisco, going to Miami, yadda yadda yadda.

Well, last week Bryce asked me when we were going to visit “Your Ammy”. It threw me for a second, what was your ammy? Apparently every time I said “Miami”, Bryce heard “my ammy” which he assumed was some relative.

I almost died laughing. I mean seriously, so cute! Of course how was he to know there was a city called Miami?? They know so much but then surprise me with their innocence. Love!

I wanted to have some sort of Super Bowl food post go up while I was gone and time was not on my side. Suitcases EVERYWHERE, tons of blog work to be completed, you know the drill. I had been wanting to share this one with you for awhile and I knew it would take no time at all so Spicy Ranch Popcorn it is!

I love a good easy snack or appetizer – emphasis on easy. This Spicy Ranch Popcorn will NOT disappoint. Forever ago I shared my Aunt Pam’s Spicy Ranch Crackers which she makes every Christmas. It occurred to me awhile back that this recipe could easily be converted to popcorn…and it’s awesome!

I love the combination of spicy and ranch and nothing is easier and more crowd-friendly than popcorn.

Whether you’re making up a batch for movie night or for the big game – you’re sure to love it! Keep in mind that you can easily adjust the spiciness level by decreasing or increasing the amount of red pepper flakes. I used about 1.5 tablespoons and it was perfect for us.

Oh yes!

Spicy Ranch Popcorn

Course Appetizer Cuisine American Keyword spicy ranch popcorn Prep Time 1 hour hour Total Time 1 hour hour Servings 8 cups Calories 1018kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 8 cups popped popcorn
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 1 Tbls red pepper flakes 1 to 2 Tbls
  • 16 oz package Original Ranch dressing or dip mix 1 package

Instructions

  • Place popcorn in a very large bowl.

  • Mix the remaining ingredients together in a small bowl or jar.

  • Pour over popcorn and stir.

  • Continue stirring the popcorn every 10 minutes or so for at least an hour.

  • Serve and enjoy!
