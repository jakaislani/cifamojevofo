How to unlock Snowman Outfit in GTA Online? All Snowman Locations

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Hey there, fellow gamers! ‘Tis the season to be jolly in the world of GTA 5 Online as Los Santos gets a festive makeover with the 2023 Festive Surprise event. The city is blanketed in snow, and scattered throughout this winter wonderland are 25 elusive snowmen waiting to be discovered and dismantled. Why, you ask? Well, because successfully taking down all these frosty figures unlocks the exclusive Snowman Outfit, and who wouldn’t want to roam around Los Santos as a festive Frosty? So, grab your virtual magnifying glass, and let’s embark on this snowy adventure together!

Finding the Snowmen

Locating these cheeky snowmen might seem like a daunting task, but fear not! We’ve got your back. First things first, revisit the spots from last year’s Snowman Hunt – it’s like meeting old friends in new places. To make your life easier, use interactive maps like the ones from GTALens. These snowmen aren’t chilling on main roads; instead, they’re hiding in plain sight in yards, grassy areas, and sneaky spots behind structures. Get ready for a thorough search, as some might be playing hide-and-seek in enclosed yards or unexpected places.

Snowman Locations

Now, let’s talk coordinates. The snowmen are scattered across the city, from Chamberlain Hills to Chiliad Mountain State Wilderness. Here are a few pointers to help you on your quest:

  • Look near the bench at BJ Smith Recreational Centre, Chamberlain Hills.
  • Check out Goma Street, Vespucci Canals, near a bush at the corner of the road.
  • Visit 12 Sustancia Road, El Burro Heights, and find the snowman in the front yard of the house.
  • Head to Legion Square, Downtown Los Santos, and spot the snowman near the red square.
  • Swing by Decker Park, Vespucci Canals, and search one of the ramps for a hidden snowman.

Unlocking the Snowman Outfit

Once you’ve conquered the quest and dismantled all 25 snowmen, the coveted Snowman Outfit is yours! Not only does this festive attire add a touch of holiday cheer to your in-game wardrobe, but you’ll also be rewarded with $5,000 for each snowman you take down. Talk about a lucrative winter gig! And as the cherry on top, a bonus reward of $125,000 awaits you upon completing the mission. Now, that’s some serious holiday spirit!

Equipping the Snowman Outfit

Equipping the Snowman Outfit is a piece of cake:

  • Go to your apartment.
  • Swing open that wardrobe.
  • Navigate to the “Outfits” section.
  • Click on the “Festive” option.
  • Select “The Snowman” outfit.
