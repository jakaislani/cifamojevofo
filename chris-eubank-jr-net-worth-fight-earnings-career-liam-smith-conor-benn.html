Chris Eubank Jr net worth: How much Next Gen has earned throughout his career as he makes retu

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 12, 2024

Chris Eubank Jr will finally return to the ring tonight as he faces Liam Smith after his fight with Conor Benn was postponed.

Eubank Jr and Benn were hoping to renew their fathers' rivalry but the fight was suspended after Benn failed a drug test in the build-up. 

As the fight between Eubank Jr and Benn did not go ahead, ‘Next Gen’ missed out on what was reported to be a £7million purse. 

He will now take a payout of around £2.4million for his upcoming bout with Smith.

But how much of an impact will that have his on net worth? 

Chris Eubank Jr net worth

Eubank Jr has a reported net worth of around £5million.

His huge fights against George Groves and Liam Williams have handed him his biggest purses to date. 

The 33-year-old pocketed £775,000 for his bout with Groves before earning over £1m as he took on Williams last year. 

In total, his reported earnings from his professional fights add up to around £3.7m.

Although his fight with Smith will massively boost his earnings and hand him another estimated £2.4million. 

Eubank Jr will also have gained worth from his sponsorship deals and of course from his father and boxing icon, Chris Eubank.

He has recently claimed to be sponsored by KFC after arriving at his first face-off with Smith wearing a jacket featuring the fast food chain’s logo.

'Next Gen' seemingly joined up with KFC after his fight with Benn was postponed, telling talkSPORT: “They are an awesome brand. I ate KFC in the build-up towards the Conor [Benn] fight and I made weight.

“So, when they asked me to join the team, absolutely! They are sponsoring me now and supplying me with unlimited amounts of KFC.

“It’s a beautiful partnership and I’m privileged to be a part of it.”

As well as this, Eubank also has a car collection worth just under £1million which includes a £215,000 Lamborghini Huracan and a £218,000 McLaren 720S.

If 'Next Gen' defeats his opponent this weekend, he will keep his hopes of a title shot alive and look to set up a bout that would increase his net worth further.
