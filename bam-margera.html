Bam Margera is a millionaire American ac tor, producer, stuntman, and former professional skater. Margera’s amateur skateboarding videos gained him prominence in the skateboarding community in the late 1990s.

Jeff Tremaine then hired him to appear in the iconic MTV comedy “Jackass.” Bam quickly rose to prominence as one of the show’s most recognizable and adored characters. Later, he appeared in a number of spin-off shows, including “Viva La Bam” and “Bam’s Unholy Union,” which chronicled his life and travels with his friends and family.

Who is Bam Margera?

Brandon Margera was born in West Chester, Pennsylvania on September 28, 1979. He is Jess Margera’s younger brother and Vincent Margera’s nephew. His grandfather gave him the moniker “Bam Bam” because he used to intentionally run into walls as a child. The moniker was eventually abbreviated to “Bam” by his classmates. He attended West Chester East High School until his junior year when he dropped out. Later, he worked to obtain his GED.
