Emma Coronel Aispuro Biography – Emma Coronel Aispuro Wiki

Emma Coronel Aispuro is the wife of Mexican drug lord Joaquin “El Chapo” Guzman. She was born on 3 July 1989 in Los Angeles, California, United States. She competed in the local Coffee and Guava Festival in 2007 when she was 17 and El Chapo exerted his influence to ensure she was crowned the winner.

On February 22, 2021, Coronel was arrested at Dulles International Airport in Virginia and is facing charges of conspiring to knowingly and intentionally distribute one (1) kilogram or more of a mixture or substance containing a detectable amount of heroin; five (5) kilograms or more of a mixture and substance containing a detectable amount of cocaine; one thousand (1000) kilograms or more of a mixture and substance containing a detectable amount of marijuana; and five hundred (500) grams or more of a mixture or substance containing a detectable amount of methamphetamine, knowing and intending that such substance would be unlawfully imported into the United States, according to the Justice Department.

Emma Coronel Aispuro Age

She was born on July 3, 1989, in Los Angeles, California, United States. She grew up in Canelas, Durango, a small rural town in northwestern Mexico.

Emma Coronel Aispuro Parents

She is the daughter of Ines Coronel Barreras, a cattle rancher, and Blanca Estela Aispuro Aispuro. Her father, Inés Coronel Barreras, is a convicted Mexican drug lord and a known member of the Sinaloa cartel, a criminal group based in Sinaloa. He is also said to have cultivated marijuana and opium poppies.

On January 9, 2013, OFAC designated Ines Coronel as a “significant foreign narcotics trafficker” pursuant to the Foreign Narcotics Kingpin Designation Act for his involvement in the drug trafficking activities of the Sinaloa Cartel and with Joaquin Guzman Loera and Ismael Zambada Garcia.

Emma Coronel Aispuro Family

She was born to Ines Coronel Barreras and Blanca Estela Aispuro Aispuro. She has a brother named Inés Omar Coronel Aispuro. Her uncle is slain drug kingpin Ignacio “Nacho” Coronel Villareal, an important member of the Sinaloa Cartel and one of El Chapo’s most trusted men.

Coronel’s father, Ines Coronel Barreras was a member of the Sinaloa Cartel. Ines Coronel and his son, Ines Omar Coronel Aispuro, were arrested in Sonora, Mexico, on April 13, 2013, on narcotics and firearms charges. On April 28, 2017, a Mexican court sentenced Ines Coronel to more than ten years in prison for marijuana trafficking and firearms violations. Ines Omar Coronel Aispuro was also sentenced to more than ten years in prison for marijuana trafficking.

El Chapo Wife Emma Coronel Aispuro

She is the wife of Joaquín Guzmán (El Chapo). Coronel first met Guzman at a party on a ranch in 2006. She was dancing with her boyfriend when El Chapo caught her eye. El Chapo and Emma Coronel married on July 2, 2007.

In an interview with US Spanish-language TV channel Telemundo broadcast in 2016, asked what attracted her to Guzman, Emma Coronel said, “I would say what won me over was his way of talking, how he treated me, the way we began to get along — first as friends and from that came everything else.” She added, “He tends to win over people by his manner of being, of acting, the way he treats people in general.”

Emma Coronel Aispuro Wedding

El Chapo and Coronel married on July 2, 2007. The Wedding was attended only by a few guests and was surrounded by armed security guards. She dismissed media reports which said her wedding to El Chapo had been a lavish affair attended by high-ranking Mexican politicians, saying, “Only my family came and the people from the ranch [where the wedding was held]. It was a small affair.” El Chapo and Emma did not travel anywhere for their honeymoon due to the international arrest warrant on Guzman.
